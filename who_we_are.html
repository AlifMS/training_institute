About

Ocean Educational Trust (OET) is a best educational and job consultant in India catering to the needs of students seeking quality global education. It is the ultimate destination for all those who aspire admission to top universities and colleges around the world. Through a team of dedicated mentors from world class institutions, we not only motivate but also guide students towards realizing their dreams. We provide, through our extensive knowledge and expertise, services that will bring out the best in you in every respect.

We believe that every individual is unique and strive to provide unbiased guidance so that every student is able to grow into a competitive individual who is able to succeed in all aspects of life. Our services are in two major fields. 1) Education consultancy 2) Job consultancy. These includes Career Planning and Counseling, career consultancy , High school mentoring and Career Development, Study Abroad Consulting, Complete Application Processing, College Essay/ SOP assistance, Visa application assistance, Scholarship/ Financial Aid assistance, and overall services so we ensure that getting admission into your dream college and pursuing your dream career has never been easier.

Tell us your dreams and we will ensure you realize them!

Whu Choose Us ?

The mentors at OET, with wide experience in the education industry, guide and nurture students towards realizing their dreams. They are dedicated to analyzing every individual on a personal basis and are responsible for chalking out a detailed plan to bring about improvements. We believe that every student is unique and thus, individual attention allows each student to tap his or her deepest potential and excel in all walks of life.

All the mentors of OET have extensive experience in guiding students towards top colleges around the world. The Career Development program ensures that students' profiles are built according to their admission requirements. Through regular excursions, creative writing sessions, aptitude building programs, and other profile building exercises we ensure that your admissions consulting process is smooth and you are always ahead of the rest.

We offer a wide range of customized, high-quality, research-based talent consulting services. We are uniquely well-positioned to help address your talent needs by leveraging our know-how, relationships and service capabilities built over five decades.

These characteristics make us best educational and job consultant in India
